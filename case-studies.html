Q: Have they been used our app during last week?

Q: What do these blue and yellow bars in tables mean?
A: Blue displays relation to maximum, yellow displays relation to sum:

  • Table on the left: 752 interactions — is 35% of a sum of all filtered interactions (752 / 2094 = 35%). It is colored yellow. And 752 interactions — is 75% compared to maximum used web-element (752 / 990 = 75%). And it is colored blue;
  • Table in the middle: 99 interactions — is 4% of a sum of all filtered interactions (99 / 2094 = 4%). It is colored yellow. And 99 interactions — is 60% compared to most active user number of interactions (99 / 165 = 60%). And it's colored blue;
