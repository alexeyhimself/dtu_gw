Q: Did they use our app within last week?
A: Yes, look there are some data charts when "Last 7 days" filter applied:

  • Sankey chart on the left displays that various web-elements have been used;
  • Table below sankey chart provides digits behind sankey chart (for example, it was totally 1437 interactions during las 7 days, i.e. "All" is 1437)
  • Two donuts in the middle show, that 97.5% of unique UserIDs who have ever used your app, used it within the last 7 days. And last 7 days is 23.3% of all available data;
  • Table below donuts provide digits for these 97.5% of UserIDs: 39 unique users have been used the app within last the 7 days and stella@example.com used the most: she triggered 108 interactions;
  • Bar chart on the right displays how interactions have been distributed in time within those 7 days;


Q: Can we see what and how much have Stella and Molly ever been used in our app?
A: Yep! Take a look at the charts filtered for both of them:

  • Sankey chart on the left displays that they used various web-elements;
  • Table below sankey chart displays, that they both used "Navbar" 264 times;
  • Two donuts in the middle show, that they two are just 5% of all unique UserIDs who have ever used your app. And their data is 11.4% of all available data in the system;
  • Table below donuts shows, that Stella used elements and features 446 times, and Molly used almost as twice as less: 256 times;
  • Bar chart on the right displays how their interactions have been distributed in all available time;
